ABOUT

INO creates dark, energetic, underground, and fractured music.

The sound originated from the sticks of Vermont. Raised in solitude. Lost in life.

This space functions as an archive of releases, visuals, and signal.

Renne handles everything video and moving visuals for the team.

Her work transleates energy, atmosphere, and motion into the CMR world.

She helps document the project in motion.

Tony is part of the visual core behind CMR.

He handles still phototgraphy and helps shape the image of the project.

His work captures texture, attitude, and presence.
